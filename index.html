RFLE

RFLE is the currency of RaffleCity. You buy it in SOL, USDC or USDT — at a price always indexed in USD. You use it to enter raffles. Then it's gone forever.

💳
Buy RFLE — priced in USD
Purchase with SOL, USDC or USDT. The token price is indexed to USD so raffle costs stay predictable regardless of market volatility.
🔥
100% Burn on every raffle
Every RFLE spent to enter a raffle is permanently destroyed. No redistribution, no recycling. The supply shrinks with every single participation.
🎁
Earn RFLE — trade or play
Players earn RFLE via Warm Up games, quests and as bonus prizes inside raffles. These can be used to play or sold on the secondary market.
